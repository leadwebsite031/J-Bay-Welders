Industrial IQ

The Coastal Defense Guide

  • 01.

    Duplex Stainless: Use for heavy marine exposure. It offers double the strength and higher corrosion resistance than standard 316.

  • 02.

    Galvanic Corrosion: Never bolt aluminum to steel without a neoprene spacer. In coastal J-Bay, the metal will "bleed" and fail within months.

  • 03.

    Weld Cleaning: Post-weld pickling and passivating of stainless steel is non-negotiable to restore the chromium oxide layer.
