Client Selection Tips

1. Check the Certification

Always ask for coded welder certifications. A standard welder is not qualified for structural or high-pressure joints. J-Bay Welders provides all certification papers on request.

2. Material Grade Matters

In Jeffrey's Bay, salt spray is your enemy. For outdoor coastal work, only use Grade 316 Stainless. Grade 304 will rust within 6 months near the beach.

3. Surface Treatment

Ensure all structural steel is Hot-Dip Galvanized or coated with a marine-grade epoxy primer. Standard spray paint is not sufficient for industrial longevity.
